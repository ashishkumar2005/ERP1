# **App Name**: Futurewise Edutech

## Core Features:

- Homepage Hero Section: A dynamic hero section with strong calls to action, including 'Enroll Now' and 'Book Demo,' designed to immediately capture user attention and drive conversions.
- Course Catalog: A comprehensive listing of courses (Full Stack, Data Science, etc.) with detailed information including duration, syllabus, and fees, each featuring an 'Enroll' button.
- Admissions Form: A lead capture form that allows prospective students to select courses and submit their information, with built-in validation and confirmation messages to ensure a smooth enrollment process.
- Student Dashboard: A secure portal for students to log in, view enrolled courses, and manage their profiles, enhancing user engagement and providing personalized learning experiences.
- Admin Panel: A password-protected admin interface to manage courses and student data.
- Contact Form: An integrated contact form with Google Maps, address, and phone number to help prospective students reach out easily.
- Career Path Tool: A tool powered by generative AI provides students with personalized guidance on the ideal courses based on their interests and aptitudes. Uses past successes from the platform to refine its suggestions.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Dark blue (#1A237E) to convey trust and knowledge.
- Background color: Light blue (#E3F2FD) to provide a calm, trustworthy atmosphere.
- Accent color: Deep purple (#512DA8) for calls to action, highlighting prestige and depth.
- Body text font: 'PT Sans' (sans-serif) for a clean and modern reading experience. Headline font: 'Playfair' (serif) to add elegance to headings; body will be PT Sans.
- Note: currently only Google Fonts are supported.
- Use flat, minimalist icons in the primary blue color, with accents in purple, to maintain a professional and modern aesthetic.
- Employ a clean, grid-based layout with ample whitespace to ensure content is easily scannable and digestible.
- Implement subtle, professional animations for hover effects and page transitions to enhance user engagement without overwhelming the user.
